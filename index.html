Privacy Notice

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Neuro PE is operated by Lucy Culliford-Stewart. Contact: lucy.neurope@gmail.com

What data we collect

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your Instagram handle or phone number
  • Your role (teacher, parent, SENCO, etc.)
  • The date of your submission

Why we collect it

We collect this information solely to contact you about the Neuro PE community and related services. We will not use it for any other purpose without your consent.

Who we share it with

Your data is stored securely in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the Neuro PE team. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

How long we keep it

We retain your data for up to 6 months if you do not join the community. If you become a member, we retain it for the duration of your membership and up to 12 months after it ends.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time. Email lucy.neurope@gmail.com to exercise these rights. We will respond within 30 days.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
